    Farmon

    With the old style of farming facing various environmental resistance, a need to adapt to new environmentally friendly farming was needed. Big Inja Farming has introduced the concept of Tiny Farms around cities. Tiny Farms are family oriented style of farming. With limited land the focus is on three animals; chickens, sheep and bees. The Big Inja Farming organisation is employing the services of IoT devices in their Tiny farms to monitor and keep track of the well-being of livestock through web and mobile applications developed by the LunaTec. The whole system was developed using Javascript frameworks. NodeJS for the backend (API) part of the system. ReactJS for the front end of the web application. React-Native JS for the mobile application. The mobile application is compatible with Android 8 and higher.

    Web

    The web application focuses on granting the farmer with statistical data overtime. This will give the farmer an estimation of production around the farm. In the case where the farmer or immediate family are unable to cater for the animals; a new assistant may be created on the system and allocated duties to execute around the farm.

    Mobile

    To make sure that the farmer is able to know at anytime the status of the farm; the mobile application will provide current sensor readings, from installed sensors, on the farm. Based on the sensor readings the farmer can know whether the livestock have enough feed/water or honey is ready for collection. The farmer can assign duties to any person to assist around the farm in taking care of the livestock when they are away.
